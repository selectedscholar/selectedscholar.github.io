How It Works

step 1

Answer some questions about yourself

What is your passion? What are your interests? Where might you want to go? Instead of filling a standard contact form, our AI-guided questions help us find you professionals and college students who are perfect candidates to assist you with your school applications. No more generic tutors who have little clue about the difference between specific schools (other than their geographical locations), never designed an app, participated in a MUN conference, been a Math Olympiad finalist /football captain/concertmaster, or even studied in the U.S. (gasp!) Consider taking our optional free personality test to improve matchmaking results.

step 2

Get matched with your perfect fit

Our AI-powered platform will use big data based on the information you provide to find you successful tutors who know your field/dream school and can cater to your specific needs, including LDs. Our site also uses machine learning to learn your preferences, so you don't have to waste time to go through a sea of tutor profiles from different college consulting firms. We have all you need in one place.

step 3

Book an Instant* Consulting Session

Choose from your latest customized list of potential tutors the ones you want to work with. Our convenient filters and saved information make it easy for you to update your preferences. We disaggregate the process, so you can hire more than just one tutor for different schools and needs. Put together your own team of admissions experts who offer flexible hours. Our tutors charge by the hour, and you will never have to pay for the time you don't need. *Instant if the recommended tutor is online and available.
